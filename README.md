Hi 👋 My name is Ernest Okpako
==============================

Web developer and Designer
--------------------------

Hi! I'm Ernest and I'm a self-taught front-end developer currently living and studying in Nigeria. Shortly after completing my masters degree, I was fascinated by the the good and magical things I could achieve with coding. The journey formally started during the lockdown 2020, when I decided that a transition to this amazing world of tech was for me. Since then, I have been on an exciting journey of learning and creating, which has only reinforced my decision to make the switch into web development. What started out as a hobby fueled by curiosity quickly became a passion with a perfect mix of designing, creating, and problem-solving. My current stack includes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also have experience working with WordPress and Elementor and I have created quite a number of websites using WordPress.
